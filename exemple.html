MooQrCode - Exemples

Simple QR Code


document.id('QR_black').qrCode({
	'width':50,
	'height':50,
	'value':'This is an encrypted value'
});

QR Code with custom colors


				document.id('QR_red').qrCode({'width':50,'height':50,'value':'This is an encrypted value','color':'red','backgroundColor':'black'});
